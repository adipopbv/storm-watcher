# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or
contacting the maintainers directly. All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly.
